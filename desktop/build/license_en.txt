Welcome to CloudVault!

CloudVault is a cloud storage application that allows you to store, 
organize and access your files from anywhere.

SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS
-------------------
- Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
- Internet connection

HOW TO USE
----------
1. Install the application on this computer
2. Open CloudVault and sign in with your account
3. Upload, download and organize your files in the cloud

ABOUT THE APPLICATION
----------------------
CloudVault connects to the Supabase API to store your files securely. 
Your data is protected and accessible only with your authentication.

By continuing the installation, you agree to the CloudVault service 
terms of use and the storage provider's privacy policy.
